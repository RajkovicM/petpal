Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of animals have you worked with before?
    At PetPals, we understand that every pet is special, and we pride ourselves on our ability to care for a wide variety of animals. Our experienced team has worked with an extensive range of pets, including: dogs, cats, birds, small mammals.
  • What specific duties will you fulfill?
    You can trust PetPals to provide your pets with the highest level of care and attention while you're away. We offer a range of services, including feeding, exercise, medication administration, and plenty of love and cuddles.
  • What will you do in the event of an emergency?
    If your pet requires urgent medical attention, we'll transport them to the nearest veterinary clinic or emergency animal hospital without delay. Our team will stay by your pet's side throughout the entire process, providing comfort and support until they receive the care they need. In the event of an emergency, we understand the importance of keeping you informed every step of the way.
  • Can you meet with my pets and me in advance?
    At PetPals, we understand that every pet has its own unique personality and needs. That's why we offer complimentary introduction meetings to get to know you and your furry (or not-so-furry) friend before their stay with us.
